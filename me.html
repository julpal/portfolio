about me.

I am a student @umsi studying UX Design. I was born and raised in Ann Arbor.


I began college with a passion for studying human behavior. Along the way, I picked up a love for design and technology. I am focused on working with users to create experiences that work for everyone. Inclusivity is very important to me as a human and designer. My goal is to spend every day learning and becoming better.
